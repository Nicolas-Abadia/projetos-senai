Dom Casmurro

This project is a new version of the book Dom Casmurro by Machado de Assis, aimed at teenagers who often use the book Dom Casmurro for entrance exams and exams. This new edition of the book seeks to be more attractive for those who read digital books, being designed directly for this medium. The cover illustration was done digitally, bringing a new point of view to the work and to the main character of the plot. The book's visual identity refers to 19th-century Brazil based on visual elements, illustrations and typography with simple aesthetics and a comfortable font for reading.
