Here are some of my Branding Design projects!

Lana Lani

Lana Lani's new brand is simple and fun, aimed at a young audience. The colors refer to the different types of sweets offered by the brand. The Bouncy font with its rounded features illustrate comfort and care.

Nick Art and Design

My personal brand was created to reflect my passion for art and design. The minimalist and simple aspects, as well as the use of saturated colors, illustrate this passion.

Rio Agility

The new visual identity of the Rio Agility brand is more minimalist than the previous one, making a connection with the sport of Agility through the representation of speed. The brand's simplicity allows it to be used in a variety of contexts. The new slogan is: “Your dog like a legend!” to attract the attention of new customers.

Prija SPA

Prija SPA is a cosmetic brand that offers high quality products for SPA, hotels and resorts. The brand offers a wide range of personal care products such as soaps, shampoos, conditioners and body lotions, as well as massage products and bath accessories.
