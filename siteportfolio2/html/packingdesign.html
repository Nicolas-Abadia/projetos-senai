Here are some of my Packing Design projects!

BLOOP

The Bloop brand is a children's tea from the company Dr. Oetker. The name was chosen because it refers to the sound of liquids, especially tea, and is easy to pronounce in virtually any language. The aesthetic idea of ​​the packaging is to be attractive to children, with visual elements that represent children, bright colors, characters and rounded shapes. The proposal is a simple and minimalist packaging with simple shapes and patterns to compose the art. The logo is simple and comfortable and adapts to the packaging colors. Black outline makes it easy to read on any surface/background.

MANJAR DA SABRINA

Graphic design of a homemade dessert packaging sticker.

Protein VEG

The packaging of the Protein Veg vegan protein bar has a simple and modern design, with emphasis on the pink and red colors, which are contrasting and refer to red fruits. In addition, the brand's visual identity brings a common approach to protein bars, highlighting the product name in large letters and an image that refers to the flavor.

Prija SPA

Prija SPA cosmetic packaging. The brand offers a wide range of personal care products such as soaps, shampoos, conditioners and body lotions, as well as massage products and bath accessories.
